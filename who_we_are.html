Our Leadership

Elite Leadership, Industry Veterans

Faheem Piracha

CEO & Principal Consultant

 

With over 25 years of diverse experience in risk and governance across Pakistan, the Middle East, Malaysia, and Europe, Faheem is a distinguished professional in the field. A Fellow Member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Pakistan and a Management Accountant from CIMA (UK), Faheem has built a robust career working with leading global organizations across various industries. His expertise spans governance, risk management, internal control, and internal audit services, demonstrating a profound commitment to enhancing organizational effectiveness.
Faheem has led numerous high-impact projects, including a Group Financial Controls improvement initiative, a Finance Transformation Project, and the successful implementation of SOX 404 for major corporations.
He has also led SOX projects in multiple large organizations in the UAE and played a key role in leading the internal control function at a major telecommunications company. Additionally, he has led projects related to IFRS advisory, internal audit transformation risk management, sustainability and operational excellence across various jurisdictions.
A recognized LinkedIn Top Risk Management Voice, Faheem has also conducted Pakistan’s first survey on the state of internal audit and developed the country’s first internal audit software, Abilite. He has trained over 300 individuals in risk management and internal control, further underscoring his extensive experience and innovative contributions to SOX implementation and organizational governance.

Leah Owens

Partner - AML & Compliance Specialist

 

Leah Owens is an accomplished expert in Anti-Money Laundering (AML) and compliance, with a strong educational background and a commitment to ongoing professional development. She holds advanced degrees in Human Rights Law and Law, along with certifications in banking, internal audit, and business risk.
Leah provides specialized compliance consulting services to fintech companies and serves as an interim Money Laundering Reporting Officer (MLRO). She is responsible for developing AML and compliance frameworks, preparing formal reports, and managing regulatory returns. Her expertise in this area has been honed through her work as an MLRO for several fintech start-ups, where she designed and implemented AML, Compliance, and Risk frameworks within a regulated environment.
Leah’s career includes extensive experience in auditing and consulting, focusing on regulatory compliance, AML, and financial crime prevention. Her roles have involved delivering high-quality audit reports, executing compliance audits, and supporting major implementation projects. Throughout her career, Leah has developed deep expertise in building and managing effective compliance frameworks, demonstrating her adaptability and dedication to the field of compliance.
