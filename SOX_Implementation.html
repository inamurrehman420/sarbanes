Full Implementation

End-to-End Implementation and Support

Ideal for large and complex organizations with fully utilized finance teams, our Full Implementation route offers comprehensive coverage from start to finish.

Develop a comprehensive strategy covering project communication, efficient implementation, external audit liaison, and post-implementation maintenance of internal controls.

Perform scoping and risk assessment across Operations, Reporting, and Compliance domains, with input from relevant stakeholders to ensure materiality and accuracy.

Conduct an "As Is" assessment to document current processes, understand key risks, and evaluate existing controls.

Identify control gaps and weaknesses, eliminating redundant activities while introducing robust controls to mitigate risks and enhance process efficiency.

Collaborate with management to ensure that control weaknesses are addressed with actionable steps and that the controls are fully owned by designated control owners.

Document “To Be” processes with recommended controls, clearly mapping inputs, outputs, control points, and responsible personnel.
